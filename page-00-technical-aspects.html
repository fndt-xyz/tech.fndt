Inertia Momentum Engineering maintains two primary online presences: open.fndt.xyz and init.projexct.com. Our infrastructure is built entirely on free hosting solutions to demonstrate cost-effective scalability for small businesses. open.fndt.xyz is hosted on Google Sites and serves as our commercial hub, focusing on technology implementation and open-source business processes. Conversely, init.projexct.com is a non-commercial research platform built with the Next.js framework and hosted on Vercel, and utilizes the wrt-cms template, focusing on renewable energy and sustainable engineering.

To streamline content updates without overloading Vercel build limits, we created this site: tech.fndt.xyz. It is hosted via GitHub Pages. This documentation hub provides detailed specifications, system configurations, and promotional resources with a design language that mirrors our research site.
